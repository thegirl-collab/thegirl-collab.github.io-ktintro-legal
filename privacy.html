KTIntro Bot – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data KTIntro collects, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • Guild Configuration: Guild ID, channel ID, and question templates (JSON).
  • Interaction Data: When users click buttons or submit modals, Discord provides their username, discriminator, and responses—but we do not store these responses beyond posting the embed.

3. How We Use Data

  • To render introduction templates and post them in your server’s channel.
  • To persist per-server setup so the Bot “remembers” your custom questions.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All configuration data is stored locally in an SQLite database on the Bot host. No personal user data (e.g. user IDs, responses) is stored long-term. When the Bot is removed from a server, its on_guild_remove handler automatically deletes that server’s configuration row.

5. Third-Party Services

KTIntro does not send data to any external analytics or tracking services.

6. Security

We follow industry-standard best practices to keep our database and code secure. However, no system is 100% secure—use the Bot at your own risk.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may modify this policy at any time; changes take effect when posted at this URL.

9. Contact Us

For questions, email support@ktgaming.com or join our support server.
